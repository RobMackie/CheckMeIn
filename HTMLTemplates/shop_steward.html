Change Shop Steward

You have several choices, but be quick (1 minute time limit):

  • Scan Shop Steward button again.
    This means that there is no longer a shop steward on duty.
  • Scan button of new Shop Steward.
    This makes them the shop steward. Please give them the shop steward button.
  • Scan your own button.
    This means you are staying the shop steward.
  • Let the page timeout.
    This means the old shop steward stays the shop steward.
